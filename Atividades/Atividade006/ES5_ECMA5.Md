### JavaScript ES5

ECMAScript 2009, also known as ES5, was the first major revision to JavaScript.

This chapter describes the most important features of ES5.

JSON.parse()
JSON.stringify()
Date.now()
Date.toISOString()
Date.toJSON()
Property getters and setters
Reserved words as property names
Object methods
